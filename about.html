About Me

I've seen many website interfaces that were designed for simplicity, but break easily. I enjoy figuring out how programs work, and I want to help make them better for the users they were intended for.

I earned my Bachelor's degree in computer science at Western Washington University, and I graduated in Fall 2018. I have had two internships in software development since graduating, which taught me Git, Javascript, and React.

Since my second internship ended, I have been primarily self-taught, and have practiced coding through online tutorials like the ones in freeCodeCamp's courses. I hope to find a team to work with that can help me grow as a software developer while improving user experience.
